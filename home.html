What is TouchLogin?

Let's face it, no one likes to remember passwords. In fact, if you are like 80% of Internet users, you use the same 1-2 passwords for every service. Now, if someone gets ahold of that one password, you lose every account that uses that password, which for many users will be every account they have. Plus, passwords are susceptible to brute-force attacks, and your only defense has been to use a new long and obscure password for every new account. That is, until now.

TouchLogin is a revolutionary technology that utilizes the iPhone's fingerprint sensor to provide effortless personal authentication that is literally at your fingertips. With TouchLogin, you never have to worry about the security of your accounts again. Bringing together the ease of one-click authentication and the robustness of biometrics, TouchLogin keeps your identity safe. Forever.

Why TouchLogin?

Convenient

Logging into a bank? Just press your finger. Buying something online? Press your finger. Paying your bills? Press your finger. No more passwords to remember, no more verification codes to enter. Simply touch, and be on your way.

Personal

Passwords can be stolen, verification codes can be intercepted, but a fingerprint is uniquely yours. TouchLogin provides an authentication mechanism that is tailored to you, and to you only. It's like your own personal, permanent password.

Secure

We take the security of your identity very seriously. Every bit of information you give us is hashed and encrypted, so that not even we can read it, thwarting even the most determined attackers. That's more than most other services can say.
